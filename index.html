About us

Marine Link Freight Services Pty Ltd

is private owned freight forwarding and Logistics Company established 2013.

We have strong operations throughout South Africa, Southern Africa, Middle East, EUROPE, Russia, UK not forgetting Asia with our professional team ready to assist at every port of entry and exit, ensuring on time service delivery for all your cargo, no matter how big your cargo or small, dangerous, perishable or bulky. At Marine Link Freight Services, no cargo is small for our huge experience and no cargo is huge for our small hands to handle; we are committed in providing value-added freight management and logistics solutions locally and internationally.

Our team of logistics, customs clearing and forwarding experts seeks nothing but profitable business solutions in an ever increasing and complex and competitive market place. That is why our aim and target is from individual entrepreneurship, small to medium enterprise (SMEs) further to governmental parastatals. With our BBE compliance, we see no skin color boundary when it comes to getting goals accomplished in the harsh economic environment in as much as trade is involved.
